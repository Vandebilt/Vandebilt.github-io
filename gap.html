Into the Gap (1983) - by Thompson Twins

Into the Gap is the fourth studio album by British pop group Thompson Twins, released on 17 February 1984 by Arista Records.[3] The album was recorded during 1983 at Compass Point Studios, in Nassau, Bahamas, and was produced by Alex Sadkin who had produced the band's previous album.
